1. About the App

Xqfz (Flame Gallery) is a creative design application that lets you compose, customize, and export artistic digital flame artworks. Features include a flame studio/editor, gallery and collection views, daily challenges, badges, flame fusion experiments, multi-flame canvas layouts, and local export of images and animations.

The App is an artistic creation tool — not a lighter simulator, physical flame simulator, or wallpaper distribution service. Visual effects are stylized and procedural; they do not represent real-world fire behavior.
