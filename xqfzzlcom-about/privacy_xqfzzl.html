1. Scope & Controller

This Privacy Policy describes how Xqfz (Flame Gallery) ("App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our iOS mobile application for artistic digital flame creation, collection, and export.

For the purposes of applicable privacy laws — including the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the CPRA (CCPA/CPRA), Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA), and Australia's Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) — we act as the data controller (or equivalent) for personal information processed through the App.

This Policy applies to information collected through the App. It does not cover third-party websites or services you may access independently.

2. Information We Collect

Because the App is primarily local-first, we collect minimal information:

Category Examples Source
App & Usage Data (local) Artwork configurations, titles, creation timestamps, editor settings, badge progress, challenge status, export counts, coin balance Generated by your use of the App; stored on device
Device & Diagnostic Data Device model, OS version, App version, crash logs (if enabled by Apple/platform) Your device / Apple systems

3. How We Use Information

We use information only as necessary to:

  • provide core App features (create, edit, save, collect, and export flame artworks);
  • maintain local statistics, badges, daily challenges, and virtual credit balances;
  • process and validate in-app purchases through Apple;
  • request and honor your device permission choices;
  • respond to support requests and enforce our User Agreement;
  • improve stability and security, including diagnosing crashes where platform tools allow;
  • comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (where applicable): performance of our contract with you (providing the App), your consent (permissions and optional tracking), and our legitimate interests in operating and securing the App, balanced against your rights.

4. Local Storage

Your flame artworks, preferences, privacy consent status, tracking request status, and usage statistics are stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage mechanisms (such as local files and UserDefaults). This data remains on your device unless you export it, back up your device through Apple iCloud/device backup, or delete it.

Important: Uninstalling the App or using "Clear Local Data" in Settings will permanently delete locally stored artworks and statistics from the App. We cannot recover deleted local data because we do not maintain server copies.

5. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions. You can deny or revoke them in iOS Settings at any time:

Photo Library

Requested when you choose to save exported images (PNG, JPG, GIF, poster) to your photo library. We access only the items you explicitly save. We do not scan or upload your entire photo library.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On supported iOS versions, we may request permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising or analytics purposes. Tracking is optional. If you deny permission, the App continues to function. You can change your choice in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Microphone (optional feature)

If you use music-sync or audio-reactive flame features, the App may request microphone access to analyze ambient audio on-device. Audio is processed locally for visual effects and is not uploaded to our servers.

Notifications (if enabled)

If we offer optional reminders (such as daily challenge notifications), you may grant notification permission. You can disable notifications in system settings.

6. In-App Purchases

Virtual credit purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive purchase confirmation data (such as product ID and transaction status) to credit your in-App balance. We do not receive or store your full payment card details — Apple handles payment processing.

Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's own policies. See Apple Privacy Policy.

7. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers / platform partners: Apple (App Store, StoreKit, system frameworks) as necessary to distribute the App and process purchases;
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request;
  • Protection: to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the public, or our App;
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protection consistent with this Policy.

In the preceding twelve (12) months, we have not sold or shared personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

8. Retention & Deletion

On-device data: Retained until you delete individual artworks, clear all local data in App Settings, or uninstall the App.

Support emails: Retained only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry and meet legal obligations, then deleted or anonymized.

Purchase records: Retained as required for fraud prevention, accounting, and legal compliance.

You can delete local App data at any time via Profile > Settings > Clear Local Data.

9. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access — request confirmation of whether we process your personal information and obtain a copy;

  • Deletion — request deletion of personal information we hold;
  • Restriction / Opt-out — restrict certain processing or opt out of sale/sharing where applicable;
  • Portability — receive information in a portable format where technically feasible;
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is consent-based, withdraw consent without affecting prior lawful processing;
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable under local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent.

If you believe a child has provided us personal information without consent, contact us at Xqfz-daijuan@163.com and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

11. International Users

The App is available to users in multiple regions. Because data is stored locally on your device, cross-border transfers of your artwork and settings generally occur only if you choose to back up your device through Apple services or manually export content.

If you contact us by email, your communication may be processed in jurisdictions where our email service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect information consistent with this Policy regardless of location.

12. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the limited data we handle. Local data benefits from your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, encryption).

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and Apple ID account, especially regarding in-app purchases and device backups.
