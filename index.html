Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I reset my account password?
A: If you have forgotten your password, please contact us via the support email above. We will assist you in resetting your password within 24 hours.
Q: What should I do if the app is not working properly?
A: Please try the following steps: 1) Restart the app 2) Check your network connection 3) Update to the latest version 4) If the problem persists, please contact us via email with a detailed description of the issue.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can manage or cancel your subscription service through your device's subscription settings (iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions; Android: Google Play > Subscriptions).
Q: How do I delete my account and data?
A: If you need to delete your account and related data, please send an email to our support address. We will process your request within 7 business days after receiving it.
Q: How do I report bugs or issues in the app?
A: Please contact us via the support email and provide as much detail as possible about the issue, including steps to reproduce, error messages, screenshots, etc. This will help us resolve the issue more quickly.
